Section 4 - Extension of Code

  • Extends IPC to offenses outside India.
  • Applies to Indian citizens & Indian vessels.
  • Covers cybercrimes targeting Indian systems.
  • Examples: Murder abroad, hijacking, hacking.
  • Punishments: Similar to those in India.

Key Points Summary

  • Crimes by Indian citizens abroad.
  • Offenses on Indian ships/aircraft.
  • Cybercrimes from abroad targeting India.
  • Relevant cases and related sections.
